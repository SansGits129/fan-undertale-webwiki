More About AUs

As you may have learned in the homepage, AUs are fanmade reinterpretations or variations on Undertale.
In this webpage, we will go more indepth on some of the most popular AUs. Describing what they're about and what's changed from the original game.

Some important things you may need to know:
Some of these AUs differ drasticly from the original game in both story, and personalities of the different characters.
That being said, it is reccommended to have played the original game before checking out these AUs for yourself. While this website does do it's best to be spoiler-free, you will probably recieve spoilers if you go check out these AUs for yourself.
Sans is the most popular character in the fandom, so expect to see alot of variants of him here.
This page only coveres a few of the most popular AUs so check out the links in the footer if you want to see more.
There is an important concept covered later in this page, Out!Codes. Please be sure to read that section if you plan to skip over some parts.

Underswap

Underswap is an AU similar to undertale, but with the key difference of almost every character having their role and personality "swapped" from the original game.
A good example of this is Sans now becoming more energetic, wheras Papyrus becomes more laid back and chill, as seen on the image on the left.
Despite this, the characters do have their own personality quriks.

This AU was orinally created by Popcorn Pr1nce, but is now Community Owned, meaning that the community and fans of this AU make the content for it. There isn't an "official version" of this AU.

Not to mention, it was one of the first AUs ever made and also one of the most popular, with lots of content coming from this AU.

Some of the swapped characters in this AU include:

  • Sans - Papyrus
  • Toriel - Asgore
  • Undyne - Alphys

Underfell

Underfell is a community owned AU that's meant to play as a much darker spin on the original Undertale.

After getting sealed underground, Asgore became vengeful, developing a deep, permanent hatred for humanity.
His hatred for humanity directly affected the underground, making everyone more miserable and fearful in the underground.
Miserable due to the horrible and hostile living environments, and fearful because one wrong move could mean death by the hands of their king.

This has caused the inhabitants of the Underground to become alot less happy and even malicious to an extent.
In this AU, you still play as the same main protagonist and luckily you still have your good friend Flowey to help you along this journey!

In the image above, you can see how each of the main characters have changed, each becoming alot darker (and edgier) than their original counterpart.

Outertale

Outertale is an AU very similar to that of Undertale, with one key difference.
That key difference being that instead of the monsters being sealed underground, they were banished to another star system in deep space.
You play as a child who's been sent to said star system and must find a way out.

This is one of the few AUs that actually has a full playable fangame tied to it. That being Project Spacetime

Storyshift

Storyshift is an AU like that of Underswap, in which the different charcters change places with eachother.
However, unlike Underswap, the roles of the characters are different from Underswap.

For example, Papyrus takes the role of Toriel, Sans takes the role of Asgore, Undyne takes the role of Mettaton, and more!

The most popular character in this AU the person who takes the role of Sans. However we won't talk about them in this article as it counts as a spoiler for Undertale.

Last Breath

Last breath is an AU/Fangame that parallels an Undertale Genocide, However before Sans' fight against the human, he comes across a Mysterious Stranger
This Stranger gifted Sans with Forbidden Knowledge and New Powers.

Now that Sans knew what was going to happen, he swore that he would fight against the human until his Last Breath.

Aftertale

Aftertale is an AU Comic made by CrayonQueen where Sans during a Genocide route realises that the only way to beat the Human is to become as powerful as them.
He injects himself with a mysterious substance from Alphys' Lab that makes him stronger and gives him access to the Save Screen itself.
However, his attempt is futile, as he falls to the Human anyways. However, before he dies, he transports himself to the Save Screen which allows him to live.
Because of this, though, he is now trapped as if he were to exit, he would die.

After awhile, he gives himself the nickname of Geno to differentiate himself from who he once was.

Dusttale

Dusttale is an AU where after countless massacres were done again and again by the Human, Sans snapped.
He began a massacre of his own, killing everyone, even his own brother, in an effort to "get stronger" and overpower the Human.

Will Sans finally succeed in stopping the Human and their Genocidal rampage, or will he end up falling to the Status Quo?

What are Out!Codes?

Out!Codes, in short, are Fanamde Characters that don't belong to any specific AU.
Instead, they're often seen travelling across the Multiverse to different AUs in Fanmade Content.
Out!Codes are a cool concept and tool often used by the fandom to have crossovers between AUs.
These characters are often protrayed to be very powerful which also explains their ability to traverse Uniiverses.

Good examples of Out!Codes are Ink Sans and Error Sans who are explained below.

Ink and Error

Ink Sans and Error Sans (or Ink and Error for short) are often portrayed as direct counterparts to eachother.

Ink Sans is a version of Sans whose main goal is to spread creativity encourage the creation of more AUs.
He is often seen wielding a giant paintbrush on him that channels his power which he uses as his main weapon.
His main place of residence is The Doodlesphere which also allows him to traverse the Multiverse.

Error Sans, on the other hand, is a Sans whose final goal is the destruction of all AUs and he will stop at nothing to achieve this goal.
He usually resides in the Antivoid which acts kind of like Ink's Doodlesphere.
His main weapon is strings that he uses to catch his opponent's SOUL.

Error often gets into fights with Ink who has taken up the spot as Guardian of the AUs.

Fresh Sans

Fresh Sans (or Fresh for short) is an Out!Code Sans who's SOUL has been taken over by a parasite of sorts.
This parasite causes him to talk and act similar to some kind of 90s teen.
He wears shades that have the word "YOLO" on them, but the word also changes depending on his thoughts.
He dresses in colors similar to that of a 90s cinema floor, and often uses words like "rad" and "brah"
An interesting detail about him, is that his SOUL is actually located in his left eye behind his shades.

While Fresh is usually portrayed as a Sans, he is actually the parasite itself who uses a Sans as his host.

Dream and Nightmare

Dream Sans and Nightmare Sans (or Dream and Nightmare for short) are Out!Codes that, like Ink and Error, are often seen as opposing forces.

Dream Sans' primary goal is to spread joy and positivity to those he can.
He often wields a bow when fighting against his brother, Nightmare Sans.

Nightmare Sans' primary goal, on the contrary, is to spread negativity and hate to all the AUs he comes across.
He does this as his power and strength comes from the negativity of others around him.
He has tentacles coming off his back which he uses to fight with.

The series is split into 2 seasons in which a bunch of different interactions and events occur.
