What is Undertale?

Undertale is an indie game released in 2015 made by Toby fox.
The game follows a child as they fall into a place called "The Underground" and come across a plethora of different Monsters. Some are nice, other's aren't.
The game puts a heavy emphasis on your choices mattering to the world around you and implements a "Spare" mechanism which allows you to spare the monsters who attack you.
The game has multiple different endings depending on the choices you make, such as a Pacifist ending or a Genocide ending.

What are AUs?

AUs or "Alternate Universes" are fanmade reinterpretations or variations of Undertale.
These AUs take the original setting and characters from Undertale, and modify them in fun and creative ways, from small tweaks, to entire overhauls!
This concept allows fans to explore different possibilities, settings and/or themes while retaining the core essence of the original game.

Interestingly enough, most of the AUs seem to revolve around the character Sans. This is most likely due to his high popularity within the Undertale fandom.

Fore more information, check out the "AUs" section of the Wiki!
